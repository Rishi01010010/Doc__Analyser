This is a rental agreement between Aditya Kumar (Landlord) and Priya Singh (Tenant) for a 2 BHK apartment in New Delhi. The agreement starts on October 15, 2024, and ends on October 14, 2025. The monthly rent is ₹30,000, payable on the 5th of each month via bank transfer. Priya also pays a ₹60,000 security deposit, which will be refunded after the agreement ends, minus any deductions for damages or unpaid rent. If the rent is late, a daily late fee of ₹500 will be charged.
